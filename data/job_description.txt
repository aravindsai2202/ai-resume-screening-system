Job Title: Data Scientist

Company: TechNova Analytics

Job Summary:
We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The ideal
candidate will design and deploy machine learning models, work with large
datasets, and communicate insights to stakeholders.

Required Skills:
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn)
- Machine Learning (Regression, Classification, Clustering)
- SQL and data querying
- Data visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn, or Power BI)
- Statistics and probability fundamentals

Preferred Skills:
- Deep Learning (TensorFlow or PyTorch)
- Experience with LLMs / GenAI tools (LangChain, OpenAI API)
- Cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Model deployment (Flask, FastAPI, Docker)

Experience Required: 1-3 years in a Data Science or ML role
(Freshers with strong project portfolios also considered)

Education: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics,
Mathematics, or a related field

Responsibilities:
- Build and evaluate predictive models
- Clean and preprocess large datasets
- Present findings to non-technical stakeholders
- Collaborate with engineering to deploy models to production
